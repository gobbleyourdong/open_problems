## Claim
Constructive proof that pure SU(3) YM on R⁴ has a Wightman QFT with mass gap m₀ > 0.

## Why it's wrong (multiple fatal flaws)

1. **Mass gap is from the 5D regulator, not 4D dynamics.** Embeds 4D YM in 5D orbifold R⁴ × S¹/Z₂. The "mass gap" is the Sturm-Liouville eigenvalue on [0, πR] — a Kaluza-Klein mode mass, not a glueball mass. Depends on the orbifold radius R (a regulator parameter).

2. **K_p bound is mathematically wrong.** Claims |K_p| ~ A·g₀² (shrinks with β) but K_p = exp[-β/3 · Re Tr(1-U)] - 1, which GROWS with β. Contradictory. Invalidates convergence proof.

3. **Weak coupling only.** Convergence requires β > 630 (g₀² < 0.0095). Physical mass gap is a strong-coupling phenomenon. The proof doesn't work where it matters.

4. **No real RG flow.** Cites Balaban but replaces 500+ pages of multi-scale analysis with a single polymer expansion and "hierarchical RG interpretation" that is not actually performed.

5. **Linearized analysis.** Separation of variables assumes small perturbations around classical background. Cannot capture nonperturbative confinement dynamics.

6. **11 references, mostly textbooks.** No engagement with Tomboulis, Chatterjee, Magnen-Rivasseau, or any modern constructive QFT literature.

## Relevance to our campaign
NONE. Our work (GC > 0 certificates, Tomboulis vortex bounds, FKG inequalities) operates at finite β in the genuinely nonperturbative regime. This paper sidesteps rather than solves the problem. The orbifold trick smuggles the mass gap in through the regulator.
