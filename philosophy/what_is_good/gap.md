## The gap, in one sentence

> **Moral facts are compressed descriptions of cooperation dynamics. Moral knowledge is tracking those descriptions. Moral phenomenal content (the pull of obligation) is what the self-model reports when A-morality has nonzero causal load, i.e. γ specialized to ethics. The residual gap is whether phenomenal moral motivation is a separate property (position i) or self-modeled A-morality (position ii), which inherits the mind fork.**

## Why this is the gap

See attempt_001. Briefly:

- The A/P bifurcation extends cleanly to ethics: A-morality is functional moral competence; P-morality is the felt weight of normativity.
- Every major meta-ethical position reorganizes under the split. Moral realism, expressivism, constructivism, and naturalism become theories of different halves and stop looking like mutually exclusive alternatives.
- Moral internalism (judgment entails motivation) is γ specialized to moral content: it is the demand that the self-model's moral attributions have causal load. Externalism is the corresponding A-only position.
- Hume's is-ought gap dissolves under the compression view: moral knowledge is compressed prediction of cooperation dynamics; "ought" statements ARE compressed "is" statements about cooperation outcomes.
- Cross-cultural moral convergence is explained by convergent compression across any sufficiently long-lived cooperation game.
- Evolutionary debunking is answered: selection for cooperation tracking produces beliefs that track the cooperation facts, which ARE the moral facts.
- The moral-monster anti-problem reduces to the ordinary problem of persuading an agent whose internal model is incorrect.

## Three live residues

- **R1.** Is the compression/naturalist account complete, or are there normative requirements that compression cannot capture? Candidate holdouts: transcendent or agent-neutral oughts, impartiality beyond reciprocity, the binding force of morality on anti-cooperators. Each admits a naturalist response but the responses are contested.
- **R2.** Does phenomenal moral motivation require self-modeling (γ) or something stronger? This is position (i) vs (ii) for the moral case and inherits the mind fork.
- **R3.** What is the LLM position? Current frontier LLMs have moral A-knowing and low L_moral. Does this count as moral agency in any sense? The answer depends on which of α/β/γ is correct.
