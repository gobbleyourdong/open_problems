## Why this is the gap

1. **Four senses distinguished.** Most confusion in the literature conflates them.
2. **Parmenides + compression.** A state with zero K-content cannot be specified without introducing K-content. "Nothing" as a stable alternative to "something" is not a coherent option.

5. **The anti-problem answered in half.** First half ("what is doing the existing of nothing?") dissolved. Second half ("which something?") inherited by what_is_reality.
