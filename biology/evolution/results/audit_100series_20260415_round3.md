## VERIFIED claims

### attempt_104 citations

**V1. Hirano M, Guo P, McCurley N, Schorpp M, Das S, Boehm T, Cooper
MD (2013).** "Evolutionary implications of a third lymphocyte lineage
in lampreys." *Nature* 501:435-438. DOI 10.1038/nature12467.
VERIFIED. Search confirmed: VLRC+ lymphocytes express orthologues of
gnathostome γδ-T-cell differentiation genes; VLRA+ express αβ-T-cell
orthologues; both types undergo VLR assembly in the thymoid gill
region. attempt_104's three-way parallel framing (VLRA/VLRC/VLRB
≈ αβ-T/γδ-T/B) matches primary source exactly.

Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/nature12467

**V2. Bajoghli B, Guo P, Aghaallaei N, Hirano M, Strohmeier C,
McCurley N, Bockman DE, Schorpp M, Cooper MD, Boehm T (2011).** "A
thymus candidate in lampreys." *Nature* 470:90-94. PMID **21293377**
(note: attempt_104 cited PMID 21293374 — see correction C1 below).
VERIFIED for all other content: thymoid at gill filament tips;
FOXN1 + CDA1 + VLRA co-expression in this tissue; evidence for
"primary lymphoid organs" in common ancestor of jawed and jawless
vertebrates.

Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/nature09655

### attempt_105 citations

**V3. Kapitonov VV, Jurka J (2005).** "RAG1 Core and V(D)J
Recombination Signal Sequences Were Derived from Transib Transposons."
*PLoS Biology* 3(6):e181. PMID 15898832. VERIFIED. The paper's key
findings confirm attempt_105's framing: the ~600-amino acid core
region of RAG1 is derived from Transib transposase; RSSs are derived
from the transposon's terminal inverted repeats; DDE catalytic triad
shared with Transib. Note: paper proposes that the RAG1 N-terminal
domain came from a SEPARATE source (not Transib) — this subtlety
could be noted in attempt_105 but is not a contradiction.

Source: https://journals.plos.org/plosbiology/article?id=10.1371/journal.pbio.0030181

### attempt_106 citations

**V4. Mi S, Lee X, Li X, Veldman GM, Finnerty H, Racie L, LaVallie E,
Tang XY, Edouard P, Howes S, Keith JC Jr, McCoy JM (2000).**
"Syncytin is a captive retroviral envelope protein involved in human
placental morphogenesis." *Nature* 403:785-789. PMID 10693809.
VERIFIED. Confirmed: syncytin is the env gene of HERV-W; expressed
in placental syncytiotrophoblasts; recombinant syncytin induces
giant syncytium formation; fusion inhibited by anti-syncytin
antiserum. attempt_106's framing of the HERV-W captured retroviral
fusion protein is accurate.

Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/35001608

**V5. Muramatsu M, Kinoshita K, Fagarasan S, Yamada S, Shinkai Y,
Honjo T (2000).** "Class switch recombination and hypermutation
require activation-induced cytidine deaminase (AID), a potential RNA
editing enzyme." *Cell* 102:553-563. PMID 11007474. Sept 1 2000.
Kyoto University. VERIFIED. Confirmed: AID overexpression in
CH12F3-2 B lymphoma cells augments IgM→IgA class switching without
cytokine stimulation; AID deficiency causes complete defect in class
switching with hyper-IgM phenotype. attempt_106's description of
AID's requirement for CSR and SHM is accurate.

Source: https://www.cell.com/fulltext/S0092-8674(00)00078-7

**V6. Simister NE, Mostov KE (1989).** "An Fc receptor structurally
related to MHC class I antigens." *Nature* 337:184-187. PMID 2911353.
VERIFIED. Confirmed: FcRn was characterized as mediating IgG uptake
from milk by neonatal rat intestinal epithelium; resolved SDS-PAGE
components p51 (MHC-class-I-homologous) + p14 (β2-microglobulin);
"first time a function has been assigned to an MHC antigen-related
molecule." attempt_106's description is accurate.

Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/337184a0
