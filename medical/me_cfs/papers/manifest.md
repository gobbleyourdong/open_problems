# ME/CFS — Paper Manifest

> Key citations needed to ground the attempts and models. Priority-ordered.

## TIER 1: Foundational

| Paper | What it proves | Used in |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| **Gow et al. 1991, BMJ** — "Enteroviral RNA sequences detected by polymerase chain reaction in muscle of patients with postviral fatigue syndrome" | Enteroviral RNA in 42% of ME/CFS muscle biopsies vs 9% controls | Gap analysis, evidence grades (CVB-ME/CFS claim) |
| **Brenu et al. 2011, J Translational Medicine** — "Immunological abnormalities as potential biomarkers in CFS/ME" | NK cell cytotoxicity reduced in ME/CFS (40-60% of normal) | NK cell restoration attempt 002 |
| **Hardcastle et al. 2015, Immunology and Cell Biology** — "Serum immune proteins in moderate and severe CFS/ME" | NK cell dysfunction correlates with symptom severity | NK cell model |
| **Nakatomi et al. 2014, J Nuclear Medicine** — "Neuroinflammation in patients with CFS/ME: a 11C-(R)-PK11195 PET study" | PET evidence of microglial activation in ME/CFS brains | Vicious cycle model (neuroinflammation arm) |
| **Chia & Chia 2008, J Clinical Pathology** — "Chronic fatigue syndrome is associated with chronic enterovirus infection of the stomach" | Enteroviral VP1 in gastric biopsies of 82% of ME/CFS patients | Gut persistence component |
