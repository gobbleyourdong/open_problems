# Myocarditis / DCM — Paper Manifest

> Key citations needed to ground the attempts and models. Priority-ordered.

## TIER 1: Foundational (must read, claims depend on these)

| Paper | What it proves | Used in |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| **Kühl et al. 2003, Circulation** — "Interferon-β treatment eliminates cardiotropic viruses and improves left ventricular function in patients with myocardial persistence of viral genomes and left ventricular dysfunction" | IFN-β clears enterovirus from DCM hearts; LVEF improves 44→53% | DCM attempt 003, evidence grades |
| **Bowles et al. 2003, JACC** — "Detection of viruses in myocardial tissues by polymerase chain reaction" | Enteroviral RNA in ~35% of DCM explants | Gap analysis, evidence grades (CVB persistence claim) |
| **Badorff et al. 1999, Nature Medicine** — "Enteroviral protease 2A cleaves dystrophin" | 2A protease cleaves dystrophin at hinge 3; mechanism of CVB cardiomyopathy | Cardiac cascade model, 2A inhibitor attempt |
| **Beck et al. 1994/1995, Nature Medicine** — "Rapid genomic evolution of a non-virulent Coxsackievirus B3 in selenium-deficient mice" | Selenium deficiency → CVB gains virulence mutations | Keshan disease attempt 004 |
| **Xiong et al. 2002, JCI** — "Inducible cardiac-restricted expression of enteroviral protease 2A is sufficient to cause dilated cardiomyopathy" | Transgenic expression of 2A protease alone causes DCM in mice | Cardiac cascade model (2A is sufficient) |
