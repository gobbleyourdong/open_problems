# Attempt 001: Direct T1DM Protocol Transfer

## Approach
Apply the T1DM protocol (attempts 001-063, converged in THEWALL.md) directly to myocarditis. Test the thesis: "any protocol that clears CVB from one organ clears it from all organs."

## The Protocol (Transferred)

### Phase 1: Kill the virus
- **Fluoxetine 20mg daily** — SSRI with direct CVB antiviral activity (blocks 2C ATPase)
- **Itraconazole 200mg daily** — blocks OSBP/PI4KB lipid trafficking required for CVB replication
- **Autophagy induction via FMD** — 5-day fasting-mimicking diet monthly, clears TD mutants

### Phase 2: Suppress the immune attack
- **BHB via ketogenic diet or exogenous ketones** — NLRP3 inflammasome suppression
- **Colchicine 0.5mg daily** — NLRP3 microtubule transport disruption (already standard for pericarditis)
- **Vitamin D 5000 IU daily** — Treg induction, suppresses molecular mimicry response
- **Omega-3 (EPA/DHA 3g daily)** — resolvin production, anti-inflammatory

### Phase 3: Support repair
- **This is where myocarditis diverges from T1DM**
- Cardiomyocyte regeneration is minimal — cannot rely on natural repair alone
- Semaglutide (GLP-1 RA): emerging cardioprotective data, anti-inflammatory in myocardium
- CoQ10 400mg daily: mitochondrial support for surviving cardiomyocytes
- Magnesium 400mg daily: anti-arrhythmic, supports cardiac contractility

## What This Solves
- Viral clearance (Phase 1): stops ongoing cytolysis and 2A protease activity
- Immune modulation (Phase 2): stops autoimmune component, reduces inflammation
- Cardiac support (Phase 3): protects remaining cardiomyocytes

## What This Doesn't Solve
1. **Existing fibrosis** — once collagen is deposited, this protocol doesn't reverse it
2. **Cardiomyocyte regeneration** — unlike beta cells, heart muscle barely regenerates
3. **Structural damage from dystrophin cleavage** — if DGC complex is already disrupted in many cells

## Prediction
- **Pre-fibrotic / subclinical myocarditis**: Protocol should halt progression completely. The heart can tolerate small amounts of damage. If caught early, functional cure.
- **Early fibrotic stage**: Protocol halts progression, prevents worsening. Residual fibrosis stable but not reversed.
- **Established DCM**: Protocol may slow progression but insufficient alone. Needs anti-fibrotic + regenerative strategies.

## Status: THEORETICAL — needs cardiac MRI data from the patient to determine which stage applies

## Measurement
- Cardiac MRI with LGE: fibrosis detection
- Troponin-I: ongoing cardiomyocyte damage
- BNP/NT-proBNP: cardiac stress
- Echocardiography: LVEF (function)
- CVB serology: VP1 antibodies, enteroviral RNA if biopsy available
