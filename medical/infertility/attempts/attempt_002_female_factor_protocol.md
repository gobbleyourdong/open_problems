# Attempt 002: Female Factor Optimization — The 90-Day Protocol

## Approach
Egg quality is determined during a ~90-day follicular development window before ovulation. Interventions during this window affect the egg that will be ovulated 3 months later. Like male spermatogenesis, the protocol runs on a 90-day cycle.

## The Five Targets

### Target 1: Egg Quality (mitochondrial)

The single most important factor for women >35. Eggs have ~100,000 mitochondria each. Mitochondrial DNA damage accumulates with age. Egg quality = mitochondrial quality.

| Intervention | Mechanism | Dose | Evidence |
|-------------|-----------|------|---------|
| **CoQ10 (ubiquinol) 600mg/day** | Electron transport chain support, ROS reduction in oocytes | 200mg TID with fat | Bentov 2014: improved IVF outcomes in women >35 |
| **Alpha-lipoic acid 600mg** | Mitochondrial antioxidant, regenerates other antioxidants | Daily | Mechanism strong, fertility data emerging |
| **NAD+ precursors (NR or NMN)** | NAD+ depletion is central to aging; eggs need NAD+ for repair | NR 300-500mg or NMN 250-500mg | Mouse data strong (Bertoldo 2020), human trials emerging |
| **Resveratrol 200mg** | SIRT1 activator, mitochondrial biogenesis | Daily | Some IVF data |

### Target 2: Ovulation (cycle regularity)

| Intervention | Mechanism | Dose | Indication |
|-------------|-----------|------|-----------|
| **Inositol (myo + d-chiro 40:1) 4g/day** | Insulin sensitization, restores ovulation in PCOS | Split BID | PCOS / irregular cycles |
| **Vitamin D 5,000 IU** | Hormone, affects ovulation directly | Daily | Universal — most women deficient |
| **N-acetyl-cysteine 600mg BID** | Improves ovulation rate in PCOS, antioxidant | BID | PCOS or unexplained |
| **Berberine 500mg TID** | Insulin sensitizer (metformin alternative) | TID with meals | PCOS, insulin resistance |
| **Spearmint tea 2 cups/day** | Anti-androgenic in PCOS | Daily | PCOS with androgen excess |

### Target 3: Endometrial / Implantation

| Intervention | Mechanism | Dose | Indication |
|-------------|-----------|------|-----------|
| **L-arginine 3-6g** | Nitric oxide → uterine blood flow → endometrial thickness | Daily | Thin lining |
| **Vitamin E 400 IU** | Endometrial blood flow + antioxidant | Daily | Universal |
| **Low-dose aspirin 81mg** | Implantation support, blood flow | Daily during cycle | Recurrent loss, thin lining |
| **Doxycycline 100mg BID × 14d** | Treats chronic endometritis (often missed) | If indicated | Suspected/confirmed endometritis |

### Target 4: Hormonal Optimization

| Intervention | Mechanism | Dose | Indication |
|-------------|-----------|------|-----------|
| **Levothyroxine** | Replace if TSH >2.5 (target for fertility) | Per doctor | Subclinical hypothyroidism |
| **Selenium 200μg** | Thyroid function (selenoproteins) + egg quality | Daily | Universal |
| **Iodine** (kelp or supplement) | Thyroid synthesis | 150-300μg | If deficient |
| **Vitex (chasteberry) 400mg** | Increases progesterone, supports luteal phase | Daily morning | Luteal phase defect |
| **Maca root 1.5g** | Adaptogen, hormonal balance | Daily | Cycle irregularity |

### Target 5: Anti-Inflammatory + Microbiome

| Intervention | Mechanism | Dose |
|-------------|-----------|------|
| **Omega-3 EPA/DHA 3g** | Anti-inflammatory, egg quality | Daily |
| **Butyrate 300mg TID** | Gut barrier, immune regulation | TID |
| **Lactobacillus probiotic** (vaginal-specific strains: L. crispatus, L. rhamnosus) | Vaginal microbiome optimization | Daily oral + optional suppository |
| **Curcumin 500mg BID** (with piperine) | Anti-inflammatory, anti-endometriosis | BID with food |
| **Quercetin 500mg** | Mast cell stabilizer, anti-inflammatory | Daily |

## The 90-Day Protocol (Universal Female Stack)

```
DAILY (regardless of specific issue):
  CoQ10 (ubiquinol) 200mg TID (with meals — 600mg total, the egg quality dose)
  Vitamin D 5,000 IU
  Omega-3 EPA/DHA 3g
  Methylfolate 800μg (NOT folic acid — many women have MTHFR mutations)
  Vitamin B12 1000μg
  Selenium 200μg
  Vitamin E 400 IU
  Vitamin C 1g
  NAC 600mg BID
  Alpha-lipoic acid 300-600mg
  Iron (only if ferritin <50 ng/mL — don't supplement if not deficient)

ADD BASED ON SPECIFIC ISSUE:
  PCOS: Inositol 4g + berberine 500mg TID + spearmint tea
  Thin lining: L-arginine 6g + vitamin E + low-dose aspirin
  Recurrent loss: Doxycycline workup + immune testing + low-dose aspirin
  Luteal phase defect: Vitex 400mg AM
  Older age (>35): Add NMN/NR + resveratrol (egg mitochondrial support)
  Endometriosis: Curcumin + DIM 200mg + add NAC

LIFESTYLE:
  Mediterranean diet (improves IVF outcomes — Vujkovic 2010)
  7-9 hours sleep (sleep is when reproductive hormones are made)
  Stress management (cortisol disrupts HPG axis)
  Avoid BPA (canned food, plastic) — endocrine disruptor
  Avoid alcohol during conception attempts
  Moderate exercise (extreme endurance reduces fertility)
```

**Total cost**: ~$120-180/month base, more if NMN/NR added.

## Diagnostic Workup (Beyond Standard)

If standard workup is "normal" but conception isn't happening, push for:

1. **TSH with target <2.5** (not "normal range")
2. **AMH** (ovarian reserve)
3. **Vitamin D 25-OH** (target 40-60 ng/mL)
4. **Thyroid antibodies** (TPO, anti-thyroglobulin)
5. **Sonohysterogram or hysteroscopy** (uterine cavity, polyps, fibroids)
6. **Endometrial biopsy with CD138 staining** (chronic endometritis)
7. **Vaginal microbiome test** (16S rRNA — Juno, Evvy, etc.)
8. **HOMA-IR** (insulin resistance even if not diabetic)
9. **Genetic carrier screening** (recurrent loss)
10. **Reproductive immunology panel** (NK cells, antiphospholipid, ANA)

## Re-Evaluation Timeline

| Timepoint | What |
|-----------|------|
| Baseline | Complete diagnostic workup + start protocol |
| Month 1 | Start tracking ovulation (BBT, OPK, or Inito) |
| Month 3 | Mid-protocol — supplements have reached steady state |
| Month 4 | First "real" conception attempt cycle (after 90-day egg quality optimization) |
| Month 6 | Reassess if not pregnant — adjust protocol or escalate to ART |

## What This Solves
- Mitochondrial egg quality (CoQ10, NAD+ precursors)
- Ovulation issues (inositol, vitamin D, NAC)
- Hormonal optimization (thyroid, vitex, selenium)
- Inflammation (omega-3, curcumin, butyrate)
- Microbiome (probiotics)

## What This Doesn't Solve
1. **Anatomical** (blocked tubes, fibroids, polyps) — surgery
2. **Severe ovarian failure** (very low AMH) — donor eggs
3. **Genetic** (chromosomal translocations) — PGT-A IVF
4. **Severe endometriosis** — surgery + IVF
