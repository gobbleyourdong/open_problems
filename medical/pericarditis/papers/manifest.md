# Pericarditis — Paper Manifest

> Key citations for the proof-of-concept trial and NLRP3 model.

## TIER 1: Trial Design Foundation

| Paper | What it proves | Used in |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| **Imazio et al. 2005, Circulation (COPE)** — Colchicine for acute pericarditis | Colchicine reduces recurrence from ~32% to ~11% | Baseline recurrence rate, trial design |
| **Imazio et al. 2011, Annals (CORP)** — Colchicine for recurrent pericarditis | Further confirms colchicine benefit; 51%→24% recurrence | Recurrence data |
| **Imazio et al. 2014, NEJM (CORP-2)** — Colchicine for first episode pericarditis | Definitive evidence: colchicine + ASA > ASA alone | Standard of care baseline |
| **Klein et al. 2020, JAMA (RILP)** — Rilonacept for recurrent pericarditis | IL-1 trap works for refractory cases: proves IL-1β is THE mediator | NLRP3 model validation |
| **Brucato et al. 2016, NEJM** — Anakinra for colchicine-resistant pericarditis | IL-1RA works: further IL-1β proof | Recurrence mechanism |
