## RULES

1. **D3 and Omega-3 WITH fat** — won't absorb without it
2. **Zinc WITH food** — causes nausea on empty stomach
3. **NAC and ALA AWAY from food** — amino acid competition
4. **LGG on empty stomach** — delayed-release capsule helps survive acid
5. **BHB during fasting window** — maximizes NLRP3 block when autophagy peaks
6. **Red Yeast Rice at DINNER** — cholesterol synthesis peaks at night
7. **Berberine WITH meals** — GI upset fasted + blunts glucose spike
8. **WHM BEFORE BED** — not after meals (diaphragm needs room)
9. **NEVER skip WHM** — only intervention that LOCKS NF-κB completely
