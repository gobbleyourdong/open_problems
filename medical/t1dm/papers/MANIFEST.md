## Key Autoantigens

| Antigen | Antibody | Role | Notes |
|---------|----------|------|-------|
| GAD65 | GADA | Major autoantigen, early | High GADA paradoxically = slower decline |
| Proinsulin/Insulin | IAA | Earliest in childhood | Likely INITIATING autoantigen |
| IA-2 | IA-2A | Predicts rapid progression | With ZnT8A: identifies 78% rapid progressors |
| ZnT8 | ZnT8A | Predicts rapid progression | Present in 60-80% at onset |
