## Friday: Set Up Tracking

Track monthly:
- Weight
- Average daily insulin dose
- Time-in-range (from CGM)
- HbA1c (quarterly)
- Stimulated C-peptide (quarterly — the KEY metric)
- How you feel (energy, mood, sleep — subjective but important)

Create a simple spreadsheet. One row per month. The C-peptide column is the one that matters. If it's rising, the balance is shifting. If it's flat after 3-6 cycles, consider adding teplizumab (Step 5) or fluoxetine (Step 1).
