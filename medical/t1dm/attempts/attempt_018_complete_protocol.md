# Attempt 018: The Complete Protocol — All Three Mountains

## The Unified Approach

No single mountain cures T1DM. The cure is the combination:

**Mountain 3 (stop the damage) → Mountain 2 (repair) → Mountain 1 (replace if needed)**

This is the order. You don't regenerate while still poisoning. You don't transplant into a hostile environment. Clean the terrain first, then rebuild, then reinforce.

## The Complete Protocol

### Stage 0: Assessment (Week 0)
- **C-peptide** (fasting + stimulated): how much beta cell function remains?
- **Autoantibody panel**: GAD65, IA-2, ZnT8, IAA — which antigens is the immune system targeting?
- **HLA typing**: DR3/DR4/DQ2/DQ8 — which genetic risk profile?
- **Gut health panel**: zonulin (permeability), fecal calprotectin (inflammation), microbiome 16S sequencing
- **Environmental assessment**: home mold testing, water quality, vitamin D level
- **CGM installation**: continuous glucose monitoring for safety
- **Baseline labs**: CRP, ESR, complete metabolic panel, lipid panel

**Decision point**: C-peptide > 0.2 nmol/L → protocol candidate (residual beta cells to protect and expand). C-peptide undetectable → skip to Stage 3.

### Stage 1: Clean the Terrain (Weeks 1-4) — Mountain 3
**Goal**: Remove ongoing environmental triggers and reduce chronic immune activation

- **Diet overhaul**: eliminate processed food, emulsifiers, artificial sweeteners. Whole foods, high fiber, fermented foods, anti-inflammatory (Mediterranean-style).
- **Gut repair**: L-glutamine (gut epithelial fuel), zinc carnosine (tight junction repair), butyrate supplementation or high-fiber prebiotics
- **Vitamin D**: load to 50-70 ng/mL (typically 5000-10000 IU/day initially)
- **Omega-3**: 2-4g EPA+DHA daily
- **Home environment**: mold remediation, HEPA air filtration, water filter (reverse osmosis for heavy metals/PFAS)
- **Stress reduction**: cortisol is immunosuppressive short-term but pro-inflammatory chronic. Mindfulness, sleep optimization, nature exposure.
- **Cost: ~$200-500 for initial setup, ~$100-200/month ongoing**

### Stage 2: Immune Reset + Regeneration (Months 2-8) — Mountain 2
**Goal**: Suppress autoimmune attack, activate beta cell regeneration

**Month 2:**
- **Teplizumab 14-day course** ($200K list, but compassionate use / trial access may apply): suppress autoreactive T cells, buy a regeneration window
- **Semaglutide** 0.25mg → titrate to 1mg weekly: GLP-1 receptor agonist, beta cell protection + proliferation signal + weight management + gut hormone

**Months 2-8:**
- **Monthly 5-day FMD cycle**: activate autophagy → clear damaged cells → Ngn3+ progenitor activation → beta cell regeneration during refeeding
  - Insulin reduced 50-70% during FMD (endocrinologist-supervised)
  - CGM alarming at 70 and 250 mg/dL
  - Ketone monitoring 2x/day (DKA prevention)
- **Low-dose IL-2** (aldesleukin, 1.5M IU/day × 5 days per month during refeeding phase): selective Treg expansion to protect emerging beta cells
- Continue semaglutide, vitamin D, omega-3, gut support throughout
- **Monthly monitoring**: stimulated C-peptide, autoantibody titers, Treg frequency (flow cytometry)
- **Cost: ~$1-5K/month depending on teplizumab access**

### Stage 2 Decision Point (Month 8)
- **C-peptide improving** → continue FMD cycles quarterly, maintain semaglutide. Wean insulin as C-peptide allows. You're on Mountain 2 and it's working.
- **C-peptide flat or declining** → proceed to Stage 3 (Mountain 1 reinforcement)

### Stage 3: Cell Replacement (If Needed) — Mountain 1
**Goal**: Add exogenous beta cells to what Mountain 2 couldn't regenerate

**Option A (preferred)**: Hypoimmune stem cell islets (Sana-type CRISPR-edited)
- HLA-KO + CD47 overexpression
- Intramuscular injection (not surgery)
- No immunosuppression needed
- The Stage 1-2 protocol has already created a more tolerant immune environment
- **Cost: ~$100-200K (estimated)**

**Option B**: Autologous CiPSC islets (Deng-type)
- Operator's own cells → islet clusters
- No rejection risk
- Manufacturing: 3-6 months per operator
- **Cost: ~$200-500K (manufacturing intensive)**

**Option C (last resort)**: Vertex zimislecel + immunosuppression
- Proven to work (7/10 insulin-independent)
- Requires lifelong tacrolimus/sirolimus
- Reserved for patients who fail Stages 1-3A/B
- **Cost: ~$500K + ongoing immunosuppression costs**

### Stage 4: Maintenance (Ongoing)
- Quarterly FMD cycles (maintenance autophagy + immune reset)
- Semaglutide ongoing (beta cell protection)
- Vitamin D, omega-3, gut health maintenance
- Annual: C-peptide, autoantibodies, CGM review
- Environmental vigilance: maintain clean home, whole foods, outdoor time
- **Cost: ~$200-500/month**
