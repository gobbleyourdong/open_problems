# T1DM Treatment Attempt Summary — After 10 Attempts

## The Landscape

### FAILED (dead ends)
- **004 GAD-alum vaccine**: Phase 3 failed (DIAGNODE-3, 2026). Single antigen + wrong adjuvant.
- **006 Encapsulation**: VX-264 discontinued (Mar 2025). Fibrosis defeats physical barriers.

### PARTIAL SUCCESS (delays, doesn't cure)
- **001 Teplizumab**: FDA approved, delays onset ~2yr. Non-specific, temporary.
- **005 Treg therapy**: PolTREG 12yr durability, but polyclonal alone insufficient.

### WORKS BUT DOESN'T SCALE
- **002 Islet transplant**: Proof of concept. Needs immunosuppression + donors.

### BREAKTHROUGHS (the endgame pieces)
- **003 Stem cells (Vertex)**: Supply problem SOLVED. 7/10 insulin-independent. Needs immunosuppression.
- **007 CRISPR hypoimmune (Sana)**: Immune problem SOLVED. First human proof, no drugs. n=1, 6mo.
- **008 Autologous iPSC (Deng)**: Both problems possibly solved. n=1, 1yr. Mechanism unknown.

### PROMISING BUT EARLY
- **009 Inverse vaccine**: Liver-targeted tolerance. Sound biology. Not yet tested in T1DM humans.
- **010 mRNA tolerance**: Spectacular mouse data (67%→13%). Most scalable. Furthest from clinic.

## The Gap Evolution

**After 5 attempts**: Gap = antigen-specific immune tolerance (single wall)
**After 8 attempts**: Gap = fork (three paths: CRISPR, autologous, tolerance)
**After 10 attempts**: Gap = INTEGRATION

The pieces exist:

## What the numerical track Should Investigate

1. **Deng mechanism**: Compare single-cell RNA-seq of CiPSC-derived islets vs native beta cells. What surface markers differ? Does the autoimmune T cell repertoire in long-duration T1DM patients differ from new-onset?
2. **CRISPR off-target**: Analyze whole-genome sequencing data from Sana's edited cells. What's the off-target mutation rate? Any hotspots near oncogenes?
3. **mRNA LNP optimization**: Model the relationship between LNP lipid composition and tolerogenic vs immunogenic APC response. Can we predict which formulations induce Tregs?
4. **Combination modeling**: In silico model of Vertex cells + Sana edits + PolTREG Tregs. What's the expected C-peptide trajectory? What dose of each component minimizes risk?
