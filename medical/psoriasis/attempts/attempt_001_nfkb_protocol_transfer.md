# Attempt 001: NF-κB Protocol Transfer — T1DM Arsenal Applied to Psoriasis

## Approach
The T1DM attempt 062 ("Gun, Bullet, Criminal") mapped 7 strategies with 30+ interventions targeting the NF-κB → TNF-α axis. Psoriasis is driven by the SAME axis. Transfer the entire arsenal.

## The T1DM Arsenal → Psoriasis Translation

### Strategy 1: JAM THE TRIGGER (prevent IKK from firing)
| Intervention | T1DM role | Psoriasis role |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| WHM breathing → β-arrestin-2 → IKKα sequestration | Stops NF-κB in pancreatic macrophages | Stops NF-κB in dermal DCs and keratinocytes |
| Cold exposure → same β2-AR pathway | Same | Same — plus cold may reduce skin inflammation directly |
| ALA 300mg/day → direct IKK inhibition | Same | Same |
| Colchicine 0.5mg → microtubule disruption of IKK | Same | Not standard for psoriasis, but mechanistically sound |

### Strategy 2: REINFORCE THE SAFETY (keep IκBα alive)
| Intervention | T1DM role | Psoriasis role |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| Vitamin D → IκBα gene upregulation | More IκBα → NF-κB stays locked | Same. Calcipotriol (topical vit D analog) is STANDARD psoriasis Rx — oral vitamin D extends this systemically |

### Strategy 3: UNLOAD THE GUN (NF-κB transcription dampened)
| Intervention | T1DM role | Psoriasis role |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| BHB → deacetylate p65 | Reduces NF-κB transcriptional activity | Same — in keratinocytes, reduces TNF-α + IL-1β production |
| Butyrate → HDAC + FOXP3 → Tregs | Tregs suppress autoreactive T cells | Tregs suppress Th17 (reciprocal differentiation) |
| Omega-3 → resolvins | Resolve pancreatic inflammation | Resolve dermal inflammation |

### Strategy 4: INTERCEPT THE BULLET (neutralize TNF-α)
| Intervention | T1DM role | Psoriasis role |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| IL-10 via WHM → macrophage deactivation | Stops TNF-α production | Same — IL-10 suppresses DC IL-23 production → less Th17 |
| Tregs (butyrate, vitamin D) | IL-10 + TGF-β production | Same → suppress Th17 differentiation |

**Note**: anti-TNF biologics (infliximab, adalimumab) are first-line psoriasis therapy at $5K/month. The protocol achieves partial TNF-α suppression via WHM/BHB/butyrate at $0-30/month. Not equivalent potency, but additive.

### Strategy 5: DISARM THE CRIMINAL (deactivate pathogenic cells)
| T1DM criminal | Psoriasis criminal | Shared intervention |
|--------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| CVB-hijacked macrophage | IL-23-producing dendritic cell | IL-10 (WHM) → suppresses both |
| Autoreactive T cell | Th17 cell | Tregs (butyrate, vitamin D) → suppress both |

### Strategy 6: ARM THE ENFORCERS (boost Tregs)
Identical to T1DM:
- Butyrate → FOXP3 → Tregs
- Vitamin D → Treg differentiation
- BHB → pro-Treg epigenetic environment
- IL-10 from WHM → STAT3 → FOXP3 → more Tregs

**The Treg/Th17 reciprocity makes this ESPECIALLY powerful for psoriasis.** Every Treg induced is one fewer Th17 that can be generated from the same precursor pool.

### Strategy 7: DEPLOY THE SNIPERS (NK cells)
Less relevant for psoriasis (no infected cells to kill). NK cells have some immunoregulatory roles in skin but this isn't a primary psoriasis mechanism.
