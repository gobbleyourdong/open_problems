## NF-kB Connection to Campaign

The campaign protocol targets NF-kB via:
1. Fluoxetine: SSRI component suppresses NF-kB signaling
2. Colchicine: upstream NLRP3 block reduces IL-1beta → less NF-kB activation
3. FMD/AMPK: AMPK phosphorylates IKKβ → reduces NF-kB activity

In psoriasis, NF-kB drives: IL-1beta, TNF-alpha, IL-6, IL-8 (CXCL8) production in keratinocytes. Theoretical overlap with protocol but psoriasis mechanism is Th17-driven (not CVB-driven).
