# Neonatal Sepsis — Paper Manifest

> Key citations for the maternal antibody threshold model, IVIG bridge, and vaccine strategy.

## TIER 1: Foundational

| Paper | What it proves | Used in |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| **Abzug et al. 1995, J Infectious Diseases** — "Neonatal enterovirus infection: virology, serology, and effects of intravenous immune globulin" | IVIG for neonatal enteroviral sepsis; correlates with outcome | IVIG bridge attempt 002 |
| **Modlin 1986, Reviews of Infectious Diseases** — "Perinatal echovirus and group B Coxsackievirus infections" | Classic review: neonatal CVB clinical spectrum, mortality rates | Neonatal sepsis PROBLEM.md |
| **Abzug et al. 2004, Pediatrics** — "Double blind placebo-controlled trial of pleconaril in infants with enteroviral meningitis" | Pleconaril in neonatal enteroviral disease | Treatment options |
| **Krogvold et al. 2015, Diabetes** — DiViD study: enterovirus in T1DM islets | CVB persistence → T1DM (neonatal seeding hypothesis) | Connection to T1DM |
