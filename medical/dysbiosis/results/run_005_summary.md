## Final High-Signal Outputs

### Butyrate Does Not Reach Skin — The SCFA Axis Is Acetate

Numerically confirmed: plasma butyrate = 0.5-3 µmol/L (1000× below effective HDAC-inhibiting concentration). Oral butyrate supplementation = gut health intervention, not systemic. The gut-skin SCFA axis runs through acetate (50-200 µmol/L plasma, at GPR43 activation range). This is a genuine clarification — the mechanism 2 (SCFA transport to skin) assessment in Run 002 was "LOW-MODERATE" without quantification; now confirmed LOW for butyrate, MODERATE for acetate specifically.

**Protocol implication:** tributyrin supplementation is justified for gut Treg induction (local), NOT for skin disease effects directly. This is not a protocol flaw — just clarifying what the butyrate component is and isn't doing.

### Omega-3 Dose: ~3g EPA+DHA/day to reach >8% index from baseline 4-5%

Quantified from Harris 2004 data. Timeline: 3-4 months to reach new steady state. Best form: triglyceride fish oil (Nordic Naturals-grade) — 25-70% better absorption than ethyl ester form.

**New interaction found:** omega-3 effectiveness is partially gated by vitamin D (VDR upregulates 15-LOX → resolvin D synthesis). So zinc×D×omega-3 is a three-way interaction. All three deficiencies impair each other.

### M7×T1DM Th17 Sky Bridge — Novel Prediction

P. gingivalis (chronic periodontitis) → TLR2 → Th17 → IL-17A amplifies CVB-initiated beta cell destruction in T1DM. Not in published literature. Specific, falsifiable, testable with a ~$50,000-100,000 pilot study. Confirmation bias audit passed once (candidate pattern status).
