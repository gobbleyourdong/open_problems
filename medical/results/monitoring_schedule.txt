MINIMUM MONITORING SET (budget-constrained)
If you can only afford some tests, prioritize in this order:
==================================================================================================================================

  ABSOLUTE MINIMUM ($425 over 24 months):
    1. Fasting C-Peptide — M0, M6, M12     (3 x $45  = $135) — THE primary endpoint
    2. HbA1c — M0, M6, M12                  (3 x $20  = $ 60) — glycemic control
    3. ALT — M0, M1, M3, M6, M12            (5 x $15  = $ 75) — fluoxetine safety
    4. Vitamin D — M0, M3                    (2 x $40  = $ 80) — compliance check
    5. hs-CRP — M0, M3                       (2 x $25  = $ 50) — inflammation baseline
    6. CGM glucose CV — every visit           (free)            — from existing CGM
    7. Daily insulin dose log — continuous     (free)            — THE functional metric

  ADD IF POSSIBLE ($200 more):
    8. GAD65 antibodies — M0, M12            (2 x $85  = $170) — autoimmune tracking
    9. Troponin I — M0                        (1 x $30  = $ 30) — cardiac safety baseline

  THE FREE METRICS (track these regardless):
    - Daily insulin dose (THE most important functional metric)
    - CGM time-in-range and glucose CV
    - Subjective energy, sleep quality, mood (1-10 daily log)
    - Weight (weekly)

  THE CHEAPEST SIGNAL:
    If insulin dose is trending DOWN while HbA1c is stable or improving,
    the protocol is working. This costs $0 to measure.

SPECIMEN COLLECTION NOTES
==================================================================================================================================

  FASTING REQUIRED (12-hour overnight fast, water permitted):
    - Fasting C-Peptide
    - Stimulated C-Peptide (MMTT)
    - Fasting Insulin
    - Lipid Panel (full fractionation)
    - Proinsulin:C-Peptide Ratio
    - D-Lactate (serum)

  NON-FASTING:
    - HbA1c
    - hs-CRP
    - 25-OH Vitamin D
    - ALT
    - AST
    - Troponin I (high-sensitivity)
    - GAD65 Autoantibodies
    - IA-2 Autoantibodies
    - NT-proBNP
    - Treg Count (CD4+CD25+FoxP3+)
    - TSH
    - Glucose CV (from CGM data)
    - ZnT8 Autoantibodies
    - Insulin Autoantibodies (IAA)
    - Enterovirus PCR (stool RT-PCR)
    - Serum IFN-alpha
    - Anti-CVB Neutralizing Antibodies
    - NK Cell Cytotoxicity
    - Lactate:Pyruvate Ratio
    - Zonulin
    - Echocardiogram
    - Microbiome 16S Sequencing

  SPECIAL HANDLING:
    - Treg flow cytometry: FRESH blood, process within 6 hours. Cannot freeze/ship.
    - NK cytotoxicity: FRESH blood, same-day processing.
    - Stimulated C-peptide (MMTT): Clinic visit, timed draws over 2 hours.
    - Enterovirus PCR: Stool sample, ship frozen to research lab.
    - Microbiome 16S: Stool kit, follow kit instructions (ship within 24hr).
