## NLRP3 Connection to Campaign

The NLRP3 inflammasome is activated in eczema keratinocytes:
- S. aureus skin colonization → NLRP3 priming
- IL-1beta from NLRP3 → IL-17 amplification → dysbiosis perpetuation

Colchicine (COPE trial standard in pericarditis) could theoretically suppress eczema flares. No RCT exists.
